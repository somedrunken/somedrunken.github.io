The Mudhut

The Mud Hut building set in spacious grounds on old Colignan school site.Close to Murray River and Hattah National Park near Mildura easily accessible by sealed road.Swimming Pool in opening hours only. Camping facilities, hot showers and toilets. Fully equipped kitchen and dining room with barbeque, gas heater and fans with tables and chairs for 80-100. Ideal for family weddings, business meetings, birthday parties in a private, secluded bush setting with camping available. Overnight, weekend and weekly negotiable.
