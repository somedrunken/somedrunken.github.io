The Hall

It is small and great

Nangiloc Hall has a beautiful river and bushland setting. Kangaroos are guaranteed in the evenings. The Nangiloc Public Hall Committee is pleased to announce the installation of its new portable kitchen adjacent to the Hall has now been completed. The Hall committee again thank all those who helped bring this project to fruition. The hall is fully reverse-cycle airconditioned and with eight fans.Seating and tables for 150 with ample parking and easy access. This makes it an ideal setting for family functions, group bookings and organised events. It is part of the Nangiloc oval complex with bowls, tennis, netball, basketball and playground facilities at your disposal. Just across the road from the Nangiloc Store and Tavern. The hire rate for The Hall, including the use of the kitchen, is set at $80 per night or by negotiation for longer use.
